Privacy Policy

Transparency about how we handle your personal and medical information.

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Welcome to the website of Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal and medical information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our appointment booking services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better healthcare services and facilitate appointment scheduling. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, phone number, email address, age, and gender.
  • Medical Information: Symptoms, reason for visit, medical history summaries, and any reports (PDFs/Images) you voluntarily upload via our booking forms.
  • Communication Data: Records of inquiries made via WhatsApp or contact forms.
  • Technical Data: Non-identifiable information such as browser type, device type, and IP address to help us improve website performance.

2. How We Store Your Data

We operate with a secure, serverless infrastructure to ensure efficiency and data protection:

  • Google Ecosystem: Data submitted through our "Book Appointment" and "Contact Us" forms is securely transmitted to and stored in Google Sheets and Google Drive (for file uploads). These services are provided by Google LLC and are subject to their enterprise-grade security standards.
  • Access Control: Access to this data is strictly restricted to Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh and his authorized administrative staff (e.g., Public Relations Officer) for the sole purpose of managing patient care.
  • No Third-Party Databases: We do not maintain a separate SQL database or sell your data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Appointment Scheduling: To confirm, reschedule, or cancel your consultations at Apollo Hospitals or other clinics.
  • Clinical Assessment: To review your uploaded reports and symptoms prior to your visit for a more efficient diagnosis.
  • Communication: To send appointment reminders, follow-up messages, or health tips via WhatsApp or SMS.
  • Website Improvement: To analyze traffic patterns and improve the user experience of our digital platform.

4. WhatsApp & External Links

Our website features a "Chat on WhatsApp" button for your convenience. Please be aware of the following:

  • WhatsApp Communication: When you click the WhatsApp button, you are redirected to the WhatsApp application. Messages sent via WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy and End-to-End Encryption standards.
  • Third-Party Links: This site may contain links to external websites (e.g., YouTube, Google Maps, Apollo 24|7). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

5. Patient Outcomes & Testimonials

We take patient confidentiality seriously regarding the "Success Stories" and "Outcomes" displayed on this site:

  • Explicit Consent: All patient photos, videos, and testimonials are published only after obtaining explicit verbal or written consent from the patient or their legal guardian.
  • Anonymity: In sensitive cases, we may anonymize patient names or blur facial features to protect identity while demonstrating clinical outcomes.
  • Right to Remove: If you are a patient featured on this website and wish to have your photo or video removed, please contact us, and we will comply immediately.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies primarily for website functionality and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics). You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some website features.

7. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain your appointment data only as long as necessary for administrative and medical record-keeping purposes. You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our appointment records (subject to medical record retention laws).
